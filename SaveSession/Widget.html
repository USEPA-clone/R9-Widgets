Important

This widget utilizes your browser's cookies to store saved sessions. If at any time the cookies are deleted your saved session will be lost! We recommend you use the download button on each of your saved sessions to create a backup on your computer.


If your saved sessions are lost you will be able to use your backup by clicking the "Load from file" button.
