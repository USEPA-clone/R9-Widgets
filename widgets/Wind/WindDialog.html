NOAA Wind Data

The data visualized are freely available and provided by NOAA's NOMADS initiative. The widget animates the wind forecast data as moving particles according to the wind vector and the speed and color of the particle correspond to the wind speed. Data for each of the models below are retrieved on an hourly basis and the forecast DateTime is displayed in the legend, and model menu. The temporal and spatial resolution of the models varies and is described below. All of the forecasts are for 10 meters above ground.

  • HRRR - High Resolution Rapid Refresh
    • CONUS
    • 3km horizontal resolution
    • Run every hour
  • NAM - North American Mesoscale Model - (Non-Hydrostatic Mesoscale Model)
    • CONUS
    • 12km horizontal resolution
    • Run every 3 hours
  • GFS - Global Forecast System
    • Global
    • 13km horizontal resolution
    • Run every hour
